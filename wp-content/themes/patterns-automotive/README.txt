Patterns Automotive is a block-based WordPress theme crafted specifically for automotive businesses, offering a professional and customizable foundation for showcasing services, rentals, sales, and more.

== Description ==

Patterns Automotive is designed for automotive businesses such as car dealerships, rental services, and repair shops. Built on WordPress Full Site Editing (FSE), it allows you to design and customize headers, footers, templates, and global styles without any coding required.

The theme includes pre-designed patterns tailored for automotive use cases, including vehicle listings, booking forms, and service showcases. It's fully optimized for responsive design, ensuring your site looks professional and consistent on any device.

Whether you're building a site for a car rental service, auto repair shop, or vehicle sales business, Patterns Automotive offers a powerful starting point with easy-to-use customization options.
